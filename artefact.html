Artefact

A silhouette of a body is used as a tribute to Saul’s Bass title sequence design for the movie “The Anatomy of Murder”.

Description

For my artefact, I used an image silhouette of a body as a tribute to Saul’s Bass title sequence design for the movie “The Anatomy of Murder”. I created buttons in a similar colour as the crime scene homicide tape that is used for marking a corpse. The buttons act as a trigger for each manner of death when you mouse over them.
